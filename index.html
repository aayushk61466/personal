Aayush Kumar

I am a final-year undergrad student at KIIT University pursuing Bachelors of Technology in Computer science and Engineering at KIIT University.

My research interests are interdisciplinary spanning across deep learning, computer vision, biomedical imaging and AI for Healthcare. I am currently working with Prof. Suresh C Satapathy on medical imaging and development of Computer aided diagnostics(CADx) systems. In the past, I've had wonderful opportunity to work with Prof. Sudipta Mukhopadhyay (summer intern 2019) and with Prof. Amit Sethi on Oral cancer pre-detection and segmentation.

I'm currently working as a Visiting Undergraduate Research Intern at John A. Paulson School of Engineering and Applied Sciences (SEAS), Harvard University.
